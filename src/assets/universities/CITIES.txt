Place images in:  src/assets/universities/{COUNTRY}/{CITY}/  (any filename)

Example:
  src/assets/universities/Austria/Vienna/photo.jpg
  src/assets/universities/Germany/Berlin/skyline.png

Supported formats: .jpg .jpeg .png .webp .svg
If a city folder has multiple images, one is chosen (non-SVG preferred over SVG).
